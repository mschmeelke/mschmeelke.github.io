Podcast & Multi-channel — CharlieHR

The Culture Ops Podcast

During COVID, CharlieHR's core acquisition channel — the blog — was underperforming as corporate audiences faced screen fatigue. Spotting the gap, I independently conceived and launched The Culture Ops Podcast, translating complex HR and organisational insights into compelling conversations for enterprise leaders.

I developed and executed a full multi-channel content strategy: episode planning, reusable visual templates, scripting, brand voice, and end-to-end promotional campaigns for each episode — coordinating with guests and leveraging their networks for cross-promotion. Within six months, the show became the #1 global B2B podcast, and by Season 2 we were turning away high-quality guests as the Culture Ops name had become a valued platform in its own right.
